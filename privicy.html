Privacy Policy

Welcome to our Password Manager website. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy document explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how we protect your data when you use our website and services.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use our website.

1. Information We Collect

Our Password Manager website is designed with privacy in mind. We do not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address unless you voluntarily provide it.

The passwords that you enter or save using our website are stored locally in your browser using technologies such as localStorage or similar browser-based storage systems. We do not store your passwords on our servers.

Information that may be collected automatically includes:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • IP address (in anonymized form)
  • Pages visited and time spent on the website

This data is collected for analytics, security, and improvement purposes only.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our password management service
  • To improve website performance and user experience
  • To analyze usage patterns and trends
  • To detect and prevent security threats or abuse

We do not sell, trade, or rent users’ personal information to third parties.

3. Password Security

Your passwords are your responsibility. Our website does not transmit your saved passwords to any external server. All password data remains within your own device.

However, you should be aware that no method of data storage is 100% secure. We strongly recommend that you use strong passwords and keep your device secure.

4. Cookies and Web Storage

We may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience. Cookies help us understand how users interact with our website.

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features of the website.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google’s Ads Settings. Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the internet.

We have no control over the content or privacy practices of advertisers.

Our Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. We advise you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of third-party ad servers or websites for more detailed information.

These third-party services may use cookies, JavaScript, or web beacons that are used in their respective ads.

7. Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain data protection rights, including the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to data processing

Since we do not store personal data on our servers, most of these rights can be exercised directly by clearing your browser data.

8. Children’s Information

Protecting children’s privacy is very important to us. Our website does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you believe that your child has provided personal information on our website, please contact us immediately, and we will take appropriate action.
